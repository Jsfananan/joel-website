Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with an AI consultant.

How do you charge for projects?+
Most projects are fixed-fee based on scope and timeline. I also offer custom retainers for ongoing support.
Do I need technical knowledge to work with you?+
No. I translate technical options into clear, practical steps so your team can adopt AI confidently.
What if we're already using AI tools but not seeing results?+
I audit your current workflows, identify gaps, and optimize what you already have for measurable impact.
Can you help us avoid vendor lock-in?+
Yes. I prioritize flexible, tool-agnostic strategies so you can pivot as platforms evolve.
How do you handle data privacy and security?+
Every project includes privacy and ethics reviews. We use least-privilege access and clear data policies.
What's the difference between strategy and implementation?+
Strategy defines priorities and ROI. Implementation builds workflows, SOPs, and training to make it real.
Do you offer ongoing support after projects end?+
Yes. Many clients continue with monthly strategy calls and optimization support.
How do you measure ROI on AI investments?+
We track time saved, cost reductions, and performance metrics tied to your business goals.
What industries do you work with?+
I work with creators, small businesses, nonprofits, and churches across a variety of sectors.
How long does a typical project take?+
Most engagements range from 2 to 6 weeks, depending on scope and complexity.
Do you work with clients outside the US?+
Yes. I serve leaders worldwide with remote-first delivery and flexible scheduling.
What's your refund or guarantee policy?+
I set clear expectations up front. If a project is not a fit, I will say so before we start.
